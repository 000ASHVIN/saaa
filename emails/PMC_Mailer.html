We recently asked our international keynote speaker, Gerry Riskin, Founder of Edge International, for his views on practice management. Here is what he had to say:
 
Where do you see the future of the legal profession heading in the next few years?
“I see it morphing dramatically… disruptors will enter the market and some traditional law firms iwill attempt to morph to compete better with them.”
 
What must attorneys, particularly those in small to medium practices, do to prepare?
“Create internal teams that are monitoring the changes being thrust upon us and tie those teams into the strategic leadership of the firm.”
 

Why is practice management important?

“Because the days are relying on the intelligence of each individual and expecting a good result are well behind us.”

 

Gerry will be giving a more detailed presentation on his perspective on the future of legal services and what firms can do now to prepare at the Practice Management Conference for Attorneys, on 22 September 2016 in Cape Town and on 26 & 27 September in Midrand.

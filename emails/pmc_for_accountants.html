Mark your calendar for the SA Accounting Academy’s 3rd Annual Practice Management Conference for Accountants, taking place on 1-3 November, 2016 at the Premier Hotel OR Tambo, Johannesburg

This annual conference, which typically attracts 150 - 200 attendees including accounting practitioners, auditors, policymakers, industry bodies, financial institutions, advisory firms, technology providers and industry experts, offers small and medium-sized accounting firms a comprehensive toolkit for developing an efficient, cost-effective and compliant practice management strategy.

 

The programme is currently being finalised and will cover the following themes:

 

  • Day One: Policy, legislation and economic and technological trends
  • Day Two: Cloud-based accounting, new software solutions, online marketing, diversification
  • Day Three: Practice management strategies - planning, building and growing your firm; client retention, marketing and business efficiency

2nd Annual Practice Management Conference for Attorneys
Taking your legal practice into the future
22 September 2016, Belmont Square, Cape Town
26 & 27 September 2016, Bytes Conference Centre, Midrand
 
The case of personal injury lawyer Ronald Bobroff and the financial irregularities subsequently uncovered at his practice by the Law Society of the Northern Provinces have once again highlighted the importance of implementing sound financial and practice management techniques with your legal firm.

The 2nd Annual Practice Management Conference for Attorneys will provide you with a comprehensive overview of all aspects of running a practice – from a legal, marketing, operational, management and financial perspective – and what it takes to remain successful, and compliant, in today’s rapidly changing legal market.

This year’s programme is now available to view online. This year we have several exciting new speakers, both local and international - see below a summary of the outstanding speaker line up. Registration is now open and if you book today you will benefit from our early bird rates.

Topics to be discussed include:
  • An update on the Legal Practice Act
  • Establishing and growing a new practice
  • Client retention and client relationship management
  • The legal practice of the future
  • Developments in legal technology and legal data management
  • Maximising long-term productivity through effective HR
  • Legal training and education: effectively managing candidates
  • Managing your trust account effectively
  • Understanding financial reporting, budgeting and tax planning to improve practice results
